# BioBoost AI

## AI-Powered Instagram Bio Generator

BioBoost AI helps you craft the perfect Instagram bio in seconds. Simply provide keywords and select your desired tone, and our AI will generate creative and engaging bios tailored just for you. Enhance your online presence and stand out on Instagram with professionally crafted bios.

### Features:
- AI-powered bio generation
- Customizable tone (professional, funny, aesthetic, inspirational, etc.)
- Keyword-driven suggestions

### How to Use:
1. Enter relevant keywords about yourself or your brand.
2. Choose the tone that best reflects your personality or message.
3. Click 'Generate Bio' and let AI do the work!
